This is who we are



The year was 2013 when a team of enthusiastic undergraduates from SRM University came together with a vision to contribute to an industry that was making tremors everywhere. 'Drones' became a buzzword and was poised to change the world, SRM UAV wasn't far behind!



Ever since it's conception SRM UAV has been courting success, our work has been recognized by organizations like the DRDO and NAL. We have participated in eminent competitions like the SUAS and prestigious events like the Indian Aviation Expo, just to name a few.



The world is plagued with problems, pollution has reached unforeseen levels, natural disasters are now a common news headline; keeping this in mind, our mission is simple, to make the world a better place by providing viable and efficient solutions to military and civilian issues.







Laurel Wreaths Over The Year

    We secured the first rank in Technical Design at AUVSI SUAS 2019.

  • 2018


    We bag the gold medal for our research on a tailless blended wing body aircraft which is capable of vertical takeoff and landing.

    We bag the silver medal for our research on Design & Fabrication of a Universal UAV for cost-effective flight tests.

    Design and fabrication Autonomous Conventional take-off and landing UAV for heavy payload testing.

    We secure 3rd prize at the National Symposium of Nascent Aerospace Technologies 2018.

    We take part in the Medical Express Outback UAV challenge.

    Ace GT (2017) is fabricated.
